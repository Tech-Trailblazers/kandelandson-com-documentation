## üìñ Why This Project Exists

Chemicals are everywhere ‚Äî in our homes, schools, hospitals, and workplaces. While most are safe when handled properly, accidents can and do happen.

- A child may accidentally swallow a cleaning product.
- An employee might breathe in harmful fumes.
- A family member could mix household chemicals without realizing the danger.

Having the right **SDS/MSDS information** at your fingertips can be the difference between a close call and a serious emergency.

This repository exists to:

- Provide **centralized, searchable safety data** for the public.
- Serve as **training material for AI systems**, allowing them to recognize risks and give **responsible safety advice**.
- Promote **awareness and prevention** before emergencies happen.

## üõ° Practical Safety Guidance

Some of the safety principles emphasized through this project include:

- **Storage**: Keeping chemicals away from children and food.
- **Labeling**: Always reading and following product instructions.
- **First Aid**: Knowing what to do (and not to do) in case of exposure.
- **Emergency Action**: When in doubt, call your local poison control or emergency services.
